About us

Spiro is Latin for "I breathe."

We started Spiro in Barcelona around a simple belief: wellbeing isn't a perk, it's a basic for all companies. A team that can pause, reconnect and breathe does better work, and stays.

So we made it effortless to bring real movement into the workday. No memberships, no clichés, no incense. Just considered, in-person sessions your people actually want to be in, run by certified instructors we'd trust with our own teams. Available in English, Spanish or Catalan.
